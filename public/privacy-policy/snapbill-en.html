Privacy Policy

Snapbill - AI Receipt Manager

Last Updated: May 10, 2026

Snapbill values your privacy. All receipt data and images are stored on your PC only. Receipt photos are sent to a cloud OCR service strictly for text extraction and are never retained for training.

1. Information We Collect

Snapbill does not require sign-up or login and does not directly collect any identifiable personal information.

The data the app processes is listed below and is stored locally on your computer (location: %APPDATA%\Snapbill\):

  • Receipt data: merchant name, date, total, tax, line items, etc. (entered or imported by you)
  • Receipt images: copies of the photo files you import
  • Notes / tags: annotations you add
  • Merchant auto-classification rules: learned merchant-to-category mappings
  • App settings: theme, language, watch folder path, etc.
  • Usage counter: monthly receipt count for free quota enforcement

2. Cloud AI Services

To recognize and categorize receipts, the following data is transmitted temporarily to cloud services:

2-1. Optical Character Recognition (OCR)

  • Data sent: the receipt photo you imported (one image per request)
  • Destination: Microsoft Azure Document Intelligence (over HTTPS, via our proxy backend)
  • Purpose: extract merchant, amount, date, and line items from the photo
  • Retention: per Azure's policy, your input is not used for model training and is not retained after processing.

2-2. Category Auto-Classification / Monthly Insights

  • Data sent: merchant name + item names (for classification) or per-month aggregates and category totals (for insights). No images are sent.
  • Destination: OpenAI gpt-4o-mini (over HTTPS, via our proxy backend)
  • Purpose: suggest expense categories and produce monthly summary insights
  • Retention: per OpenAI's API policy, your input is not used for model training.

2-3. Fully Local Mode

You may switch to local-only OCR in Settings, which uses Windows built-in OCR with no cloud transmission (accuracy on Korean receipts may be reduced).

3. How We Use Information

The data collected/processed is used solely for:

  • Receipt text recognition and structured data extraction
  • Expense category auto-classification and monthly summary insights
  • Duplicate receipt detection
  • Maintaining your settings and enforcing free / Premium quotas

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All receipt data and images are stored locally on your PC only.
  • Cloud transmissions are encrypted with HTTPS and not retained externally after processing.
  • You can completely remove all local data by uninstalling the app or deleting the %APPDATA%\Snapbill\ folder.
  • Settings → "Reset all data" wipes all in-app data with one click.
  • Please keep your operating system and security software up to date.

5. Payment Information

Premium subscriptions are handled by Microsoft Store. Payment methods, billing details, and transaction history are processed entirely by Microsoft. Snapbill has no access to this information.

You can manage and refund your subscription directly through your Microsoft Store account.

6. Third-Party Services

Snapbill relies on the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policy:

  • Microsoft Azure Document Intelligence — receipt OCR (image transmission)
  • OpenAI — category classification and monthly insights (text metadata only)
  • Cloudflare Workers — our own backend that proxies the two APIs above (data not stored)
  • Microsoft Store — app distribution and payment processing

Snapbill does not use any advertising networks, analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics), or user-tracking SDKs.

7. Your Rights

  • You can delete all local data at any time (uninstall or delete the data folder).
  • Settings → Export backup lets you save and transfer all your data as a JSON file.
  • Settings → Reset all data wipes the app's data in one action.
  • For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at the email below.

8. Children's Privacy

Snapbill is not directed at children under 13, and does not collect identifiable personal information from any user.

9. Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, we will reflect the change via the "Last Updated" date on this page. For material changes, we may also notify you via the app.
